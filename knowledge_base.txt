OneCard offers 5× reward points on the top 2 spend categories each calendar month.
Forex markup fee: 1% on card transactions processed in currencies other than INR (no additional dynamic FX spread).
Interest-free period: up to 48 days depending on billing and payment date.
Late payment fee: ₹500 flat + interest on outstanding after due date.
Minimum payment due: 5% of statement balance or ₹500, whichever is higher.
Card replacement fee (metal): ₹3,000; standard plastic replacement is free if within warranty.
Dispute window: Transactions can be disputed within 60 days from transaction date; supporting docs may be required.
Cash withdrawal fee: 2.5% of withdrawn amount + applicable interest from the date of withdrawal.
