OneCard is a premium metal credit card.
You earn 5x reward points on top 2 categories of spend.
Forex markup is 1% which is one of the lowest in the market.
You can block your card instantly via the app if it is lost or stolen.
To dispute a transaction, go to the transaction details and tap 'Dispute'.
There are no annual fees for the lifetime free variant.
Late payment charges are 2.5% of the outstanding amount.
You can redeem points for cash credit or merchandise.
Contact support at 1800-ONECARD for any issues.
The interest-free period is up to 48 days.
